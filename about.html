Hello there, fellow ocean enthusiast! I'm Charlie, the creative mind behind Aqua Framed. Ever since I can remember, I've been drawn to the mesmerizing world beneath the waves. My journey as an artist began with a deep passion for marine life and a desire to share its beauty with others. Combining my love for art and my fascination with the ocean, I founded Aqua Framed as a platform to showcase the enchanting wonders of underwater life. With a background in both art and marine biology, I strive to infuse scientific accuracy and artistic expression into each piece I create. When I'm not in my studio crafting ocean-inspired masterpieces, you can find me exploring coral reefs, diving alongside marine creatures, or simply lounging on the beach with a sketchbook in hand. My goal is to not only create beautiful artwork but also to raise awareness about the importance of ocean conservation and inspire others to protect our seas for generations to come. Things that interest me

Below are a list of things that interest me


  • Marine Biology: As an ocean enthusiast, you're likely fascinated by the intricate ecosystems and diverse marine life found beneath the waves. From studying coral reefs to learning about deep-sea creatures, the mysteries of the ocean never cease to amaze you.
  • Artistic Expression: Your passion for art drives you to express the beauty of the ocean through your creations. Whether it's painting, sculpting, or photography, you find joy in capturing the essence of marine life and sharing it with others.
  • Ocean Conservation: Protecting the oceans is close to your heart. You're passionate about raising awareness about issues such as plastic pollution, overfishing, and habitat destruction, and you actively support efforts to preserve marine ecosystems for future generations.
  • Scuba Diving and Snorkeling: Exploring the underwater world is a thrilling adventure for you. Whether you're diving into crystal-clear coral reefs or snorkeling alongside gentle sea turtles, you cherish every moment spent beneath the surface.
  • Travel and Exploration: You have a thirst for adventure and love to embark on journeys to new and exotic destinations. Whether it's sailing the open seas, exploring remote islands, or diving in far-flung underwater caves, you're always seeking out new experiences and discoveries.
  • Environmental Activism: Making a positive impact on the planet is important to you. You're involved in environmental activism efforts, whether it's participating in beach cleanups, supporting sustainable practices, or advocating for policy changes to protect the oceans.

Likes and Dislikes

Things that I like and Dislike if you care :DD

   
LIKES DISLIKES
  • Sunsets by the Sea: There's nothing quite like watching the sun dip below the horizon, casting a warm, golden glow over the ocean. Whether you're sitting on the shore or sailing on calm waters, sunsets by the sea never fail to mesmerize you.
  • Beachcombing: Strolling along the shoreline, searching for seashells, driftwood, and other treasures washed up by the tide, is one of your favorite pastimes. Each beachcombing excursion is a chance to connect with nature and uncover hidden gems from the sea.
  • Seafood Cuisine: From succulent lobster and buttery scallops to fresh-caught fish and tangy ceviche, you have a palate for the flavors of the ocean. Whether you're dining at a seaside restaurant or preparing a seafood feast at home, you savor every bite of ocean-inspired cuisine.
  • Ocean Sounds: The rhythmic sound of waves crashing against the shore is music to your ears. Whether you're listening to recordings of ocean waves to relax or sleeping to the soothing sound of a white noise machine mimicking the ocean, the gentle sounds of the sea bring you comfort and peace.
  • Seaside Stargazing: Clear nights by the coast offer unparalleled views of the night sky. Whether you're lying on a blanket on the beach or sitting on a dock with your feet dangling over the water, gazing up at the stars and listening to the sounds of the ocean is a magical experience.

  • Pollution: You have a strong aversion to seeing litter, plastic waste, and other pollutants mar the beauty of the ocean. Pollution not only harms marine life but also disrupts the delicate balance of marine ecosystems, and you're passionate about taking action to combat it.
  • Coral Bleaching: Witnessing the devastating effects of coral bleaching due to factors like rising sea temperatures and pollution deeply troubles you. Seeing once-vibrant coral reefs fade and die off is a stark reminder of the urgent need for conservation efforts to protect these fragile ecosystems.
  • Overfishing: The depletion of fish populations due to overfishing is a concern close to your heart. You recognize the importance of sustainable fishing practices and are troubled by the impact that overfishing has on marine biodiversity and food security for coastal communities.
  • Habitat Destruction: Destruction of marine habitats such as mangrove forests, seagrass meadows, and coral reefs saddens you deeply. These habitats are vital for supporting diverse marine life and protecting coastlines from erosion, and you advocate for their preservation and restoration.
  • Noise Pollution: The intrusion of human-made noises, such as boat engines, sonar, and industrial activity, disrupts the natural soundscape of the ocean and interferes with the communication and behavior of marine animals. You find this disruption unsettling and believe in the importance of minimizing noise pollution in marine environments.
